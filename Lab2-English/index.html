Tuwaiq Academy s an educational institution based in Riyadh . The Saudi Federation for Cybersecurity, Programming and Drones announced its establishment in August 2019 and it is based in [Princess Noura bint Abdul Rahman University](https://www.pnu.edu.sa/ar/Pages/UniversityHistory.aspx ) The Academy is the first for advanced technologies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, by providing a high level of training programs on advanced technology and camps in programming, software engineering, data science, and cybersecurity It seeks to reduce the gap between the requirements of the labor market and the skills of individuals in the fields of Information technology, focusing on capacity building, providing new experiences, and developing skills. (./Tuwaiq.gif) Strategic partnerships The Saudi Federation for Cybersecurity, Programming and Drones has led several strategic partnerships represented by Tuwaiq Academy with a number of international technical companies. partnerships with Apple , Cisco , Amazon Web Services , Alibaba Cloud , Oracle , IBM , Microsoft , Offensive Security and Trend Micro .
